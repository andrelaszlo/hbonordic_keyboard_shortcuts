This extension enables keyboard shortcuts for HBO Nordic.

Shortcuts

  • Full-screen: F
  • Play/pause: Space
  • Toggle mute: M
  • Skip forward/backward: Left and right arrow keys
  • Volume up/down: Up and down arrow keys
